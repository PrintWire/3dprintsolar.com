About 3D Print Solar

Covering additive manufacturing for photovoltaics — from printed cell components to the racking, trackers, and enclosures that surround them.

OUR STORY

Solar at the Frontier of Fabrication

3D Print Solar covers the full spectrum of additive manufacturing for photovoltaics — from printed cell metallization and encapsulants to the mounting brackets, tracker drives, junction boxes, and enclosures that make up the balance of system.

Our editorial team includes PV engineers, installers, and materials scientists with real project experience across rooftop and utility-scale solar, weatherable polymers, and design-to-fabrication workflows.

PURPOSE

Our Mission

To cover solar AM from the printed contact finger to the full mounting system with equal depth — the cell, the hardware, and the material behind them.

Our Vision

To be the authoritative, vendor-independent source for additive manufacturing in solar — connecting installers and developers to the suppliers reshaping how PV hardware gets made.
